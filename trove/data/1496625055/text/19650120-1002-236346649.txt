 Tributes to Regro woman JONDON: Many West Indians were among the 400 people who mourned woman . Negro leader Claudia Jones at her funeral. Paul Robeson was among those paying tribute. Miss Jones was a lender of the U.S. Negro struggle until exiled to Britain by the McCarthyites. In a cable U.S. C.P. spokesman Gus Hail and Henry Winston said: "Progressive American will always cherish the name of Claudia Jones, a passionate voice for justice and freedom."
