 Prime Minister The Thistle Knight does legislate. That he alone may keep the gate. If you reach there sad and lonely You'll find a notice, "Members Only" — "Where shall 1 go 1 pray you tell—" "O yes! you hoys just go to Hell!" Now gaze upon the noble knight Who never yet has seen a fight, Whose path is strewn with red, red roses — whose laws surpass the ancient Moses. —CALLED UP.
