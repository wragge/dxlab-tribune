 Especially for children SYDNEY: A film matinee to cele brate International Children's Day will be held at the Union Theatre, Sydney University, Parra- matta Koad, Sydney, on Saturday, May 29 at 2 p.m. The main feature will be "MARIA THE MAGIC WEAVER". This is a Soviet colour film that is based on Russian folklore. The wonderful fan tasy of this production will appeal to all children. Also showing will be "Aboriginal Legends" an Australian colour car toon, Australian folk songs and films from Czechoslovakia and Poland. Admission: Adults 5/-, children 3/-. Buses pass the door.
