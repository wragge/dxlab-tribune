 JEWISH LEADER DEFENDS USSR gYDNEY: Unjustified accusations were being made against the Soviet Union over the Jewish ques tion, Dr. Nahum Goldman, President of the World Jewish Congress, said at a press conference in New York this month, according to the Sydney "Jew ish News" (18/6/65). "'Too often the problem is being distorted in its character," he said. -To compare the Soviet Government with the Nazis is a hideous distortion and highly unfair to Russia who saved hundreds of thousands of Jews escap ing the Nazis at beginning of the world war, he said.
