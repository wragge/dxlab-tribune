 Demonstration I against National Service MELBOURNE: Twenty mothers travelled the 60- odd miles to Puckapunyal on Sunday to raise the banner of the Save Our Sons anti- conscription movement at the passing out of 1200 na tional service trainees. A military policeman at the army camp ordered them off "Common wealth territory", and threatened to cr.ll civil police unless they moved. The women strung themselves out at intervals along the road outside the camp with banners such as ''Save Our Sons", "No Conscription for Overseas Service", "Keep Our Sons Out of Vietnam", and "Thou Shalt Not Kill". The demonstration lasted from 10.30 to 2.30.
