 Union protest' over Ben Bella SYDNEY: The Ship Painters' and Dockers' Union (N.S.W. Branch) has sent to the Algerian Government a letter expressing the "strongest protest" over the arrest and secret imprisonment of Ahmed Ben Bella, President of Algeria until the recent armed coup. The union "demands that per mission be granted to Ben Bella to be visited, particularly by his mother, to he assisted by lawyers of his own choosing and to be examined by his doctor, in a word that he be guaranteed the elemen tary rights of a human being."
