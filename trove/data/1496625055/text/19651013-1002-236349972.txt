 SEAT O POWERS SHY OF VIETNAM "CANBERRA; Replying to Mr Hay- den, Labor, Queensland, External Affairs Minister Hasluck admitted last week that most SEATO nations were not sending troops to Vietnam. He said that the US, Australia and New Zealand were sending combat troops. Britain, France, Pakistan, the Phillipines and Thailand were not. Last week British premier Wilson, told the Labor Party conference that Britain had refused a US request to send troops. He did not claim, as did Menzies in respect of Australia, that the request had come from the south Vietnamese "government."
