 TRIBUNE FAIR THIS SATURDAY The Tribune Fair will be held this Saturday, October 23, at the Jim Healy Memorial Hall, 168 Day Street, Sydney, opening at 9 am. THERE will be a varied as- sortment of goods on sale, including children's clothes, toys, preserves, jams, pick les, cakes, vegetables and plants. Second-hand cloth ing also will be available. The Pair Committee appeals for all goods to be delivered to Day Street by Friday night, when final prepara tions will be made for the opening on Saturday. This is a good opportunity to pur chase real bargains and at the same, time help Tribune financially. You're sure to meet many old and new friends on Saturday. R.M.
