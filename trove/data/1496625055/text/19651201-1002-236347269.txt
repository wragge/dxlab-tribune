 Funeral parlors CULL support for H. G. Begg's letter in Tribune (10/11/65) "NATIONALISE THE FUNERAL PARLORS". I have spoken to many people in regard to this matter, and all agree that the charges are outrageous, and a general remark is that it is "too dear to die." Here- is a question that local gov ernments could well be interested in. Jim Sharrock (Redfern).
