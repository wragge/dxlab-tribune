 A MERRY XMAI...AIID A HAPPY HEW YEAR! TRIBUNE EDITORIAL extends to all readers, sellers, contributors— all who have helped our paper— the very best wishes for the festive season and for a peaceful and happy New Year. This is the last issue of Tribune for 1935. The first issue for 1966 will he on Wednesday, January 5. The editorial offices will close on December 22 and reopen on Deecmber 30. We look forward to continued and improved co-operation in 1966, when our "new look" Tribune will be available.
