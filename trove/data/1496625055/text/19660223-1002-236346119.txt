 From RUPERT LOCKWOOD in Moscow. The Sydney Morning Herald recently disclosed that 32 million gallons of untreated sewage is discharged every day into the ocean near Manly Beach. Manly and other beaches are continually patrolled by inspectors ready to dispose of stranded sewage. Work on a treatment plant has been suspended for lack of funds. In this article from Moscow, Mr. Rupert Lockwood tells of a country with a different attitude to this notorious menace to health.
