 WA wharfies give support FREMANTLE: Last week's stop work meeting of whar fies in the port of Fremantle to hear a report by General secretary ritzgibbon on the position in the industry, also passed resolutions for a 6hr. minimum attendance money due to rising prices and lack of work; that meal money be raised to $1.50 to meet the needs of increased prices. They declared support for the ACTU in its present application for basic wage and margins in creases.
