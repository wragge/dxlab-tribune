 Death of Johnny Mocken PERTH: The death of Johnny Mocken after a long Illness will be mourned by the many working people who Knew mm as a staunch mate and friend in times of trouble. Born in New Zealand, he sailed on the "Bambra" on the W.A. coast for many years, be ing elected to the executive of the Seamen's Union. Joining the Communist Party soon after it was formed in -W.A., Johnny was elected to one- of the first delegations to the Soviet Union in 1930. Later, as a goldminer, he was elected to the executive of the AWU.
