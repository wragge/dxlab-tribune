 BUILDING WORKERS TALK AMALGAMATION SYDNEY: A conference of Builders' Labourers' Union delegates recently in Sydney decided to initiate discussion among the members to seek the formation of an association between the state branches of their union and the Building Workers' Indus- trial Union. Officials from the B.W.I.U. and the Operative Painters' Union also attended the conference. Mr. L. Boyce, N.S.W. president of the Operative Painters' Union (pictured) addressed the 60 delegates on the recent talks between his union and the B.W.I.U. to seek amalgamation on a federal level. The delegates also unanimously endorsed , a proposal that the union establish an anti-conscription committee.
