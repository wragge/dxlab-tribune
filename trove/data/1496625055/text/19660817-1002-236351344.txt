 Pensioners pressurise Parliament MEWCASTLE: Over 40 pensioners left here by bus yesterday (Tuesday) to take up their demand with politicians in Canberra for a pension of at least half the basic wage. A mass delegation x of 500 pensioners at the Newcastle City Hall had earlier decided to send the delegation to Can berra. The rally was called by the Newcastle Trades Hall Council at the request of the Miners' Federation. Commenting on the rally, THC secretary, Mr. A. Dowli.ng said it was the largest and most determined gathering of pensioners ever held in the district.
