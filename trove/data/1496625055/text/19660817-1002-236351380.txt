 Ball time Saturday night CjYDNEY: Everything was ready for the most enjoyable evening at the Tribune Ball next Saturday night, Ball organiser Gloria Sanderson said this week. The Ball is being held at the State Ballroom in Market Si. (opposite Farmers'), which Mrs. Sanderson described as "one of the most attractive and comfortable halls available for such occasions." A. first-class orchestra, playing both old-time and modern, would be on hand, and an excellent supper would bo served, she said. Mrs. Sanderson urged all those who had not yet ordered tickets, costing $3 each, to do so by ringing 28-2161 before noon on Thursday, so that supper bookings could ho made. "We wouldn't want anyone to go hungry," she said.
