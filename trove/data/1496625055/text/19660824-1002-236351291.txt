 JUNIOR TRIBUNE |a|ELLO, Tribune Juniors, School holidays are fast coming around again J (aren't you lucky) so here is a holiday competition for you. What is David: 1. Looking at through his window? Perhaps it is the scene you can see from your window. OR 2. What is David dreaming about? There will be 50 cents prize for the winning Senior entry (from 12 years) and 50 cents for the winning Junior entry. Stamps will be sent to all entrants. Your entry can be in black and white or color and should be sent to Tribune, 168 Day Street, Sydney, no later than Tuesday, September 6, 1966. A little reminder, don't for get to put your name, address- and age on your entry. Dinewan.
