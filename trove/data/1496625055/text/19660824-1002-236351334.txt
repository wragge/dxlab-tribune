 Certificate for McCalman A COMMUNIST Party member, Mr. M. J. Mc Calman, formerly an alder man on Penrith City Coun- cil representing St. Marys, has been presented with the Local Government Ser vice Certificate. Mr. McCalman was on the Council for nine years from 1953 to 1962. The Certificate was presented by the Mayor of Penrith, Aid. W. Chapman on behalf of the Local Government Association. During the 22 years Mr. McCalman resided in St. . Marys, he acted in an ex ecutive capacity in a num ber of local organisations.
