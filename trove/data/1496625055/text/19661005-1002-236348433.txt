 Mail sorters want regulations strike OTOPWORK meetings of mail sorters in Sydney and Mel bourne have called for a nation wide work to regulation strike in protest against operating electronic mail sorting mach ines at reduced wage rates. Melbourne . mail sorters last week backed this proposal made earlier by the Sydney sorters. This protest action follows a new classification for this work recently brought down by the Public Service Arbitrator. For a male mail sorter the the rate is $200 a year less than his present rate and $402 lower still for a female, It is believed that the ACTU Executive last week in Mel bourne asked federal officials of the PWU to define what they think should be done and that the ACTU will review the position in November.
