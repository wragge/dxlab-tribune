 New N.Z. Party gets under way QYDNEY: A conference held in Auckland, New Zea land on October 1 and 2, de cided to form the Socialist Unity Party of New Zealand. The conference elected a national committee and ap pointed commissions to draw up a party program and con stitution. This was reported last week end to the central committee of the Communist Party of Australia by a member of its secretariat, , Mr. Claude Jones, who attended the New Zealand conference as an observer. Following Mr. Jones' outline of the conference, the Central Committee instructed its Poli tical Committee to continue discussions with the Socialist Unity Party of. N.Z., "in order to strengthen the struggle for peace and. socialism in our part of the world."
