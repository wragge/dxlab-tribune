 SOCIALIST GERMANY'S SEVENTEEN YEARS Seventeen years after the foundation of the German Demo cratic Republic in October, 1949, this is what socialism has made of the former Frankfurter Allee, before the War a notorious slum area. Today, renamed Karl Marx Allee, it gives the increasing number of foreign visitors their first glimpse of the remarkable recovery made by the GDR, which has now become the seventh most powerful industrial nation in the world.
