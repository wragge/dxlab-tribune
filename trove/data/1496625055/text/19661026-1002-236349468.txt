 Union heat on Stewart & Lloyds NEWCASTLE: The drive for 100 per cent unionism in the Newcastle district is gathering momentum, with Union officials first concentrating on the steel firm of Stewart and Lloyds. A team of officials and delegates who checked three . shifts on October 17 are extremely pleased with the results. The FIA now claim nearly 100 per cent unionism on this job and craft unions have an almost total percentage. The Clerks' union has a non-union problem in the offices of the firm and may seek the support of other unions. Union leaders say the publicity given to the campaign was helpful and unions plan further activities with other industries. The 100 per cent union drive is being backed by the Newcastle Trades Hall Council.
