 BRISBANE lumbered TN Brisbane, a demonstration and meeting against the war and against conscription packed King George Square (in front of the City Hall) with thousands of people, includ ing some pro- war elements. Some young people lay on the road, with placards, in front of the motorcade in Queen Street. They were moved bodily by police to the side of the road. A headlight of President Johnson's car was smashed by the flailing leg of one demon strator as he was shifted by police. A girl put her face at the window of the car alongside President Johnson and made a pungent comment to him. Seven people were arrested on various charges, as demonstrators.
