 CAPTAIN OF BAJMAK This is 36-year-old Captain Romanitchev, skipper of the 18,000 ton Soviet freighter Bajmak which left Sydney with a cargo of wool on Monday last. He has been a seamen for 13 years and a captain for three. Young as he is, there were many ship's captains in the Soviet Union younger than himseli, he said. Recently the Bajmak took a cargo of equipment and supplies to North Vietnam.
