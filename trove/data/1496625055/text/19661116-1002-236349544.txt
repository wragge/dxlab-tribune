 Building crisis on South Coast OLLONGONG: The South Coast Labor Council have launched a campaign against the crisis in the building and allied industries. Brickmakers' Union delegate Mr. J. Steel told the council that local brickmaking firms are putting thousands on the grass each week bringing the total at grass to four million bricks. Since last February the union has lost 100 members on the coast. Building Workers' Industrial Union organiser, Mr. J. Clare said . that unemployment was rife in the building industry at a time when there is a chronic shortage of homes, schools and hospitals on the Coast.
