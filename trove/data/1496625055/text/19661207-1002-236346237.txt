 Congo's half-share in Katanga Co. |MOSCOW: The notorious Union Miniere du Haut Katanga, the great diamond, copper and uranium trust that for so long held the Congo to ransom, will cease its Conern activities on December 31. The company will then be replaced by Union Miniere du Haut Congo, in which the Congo (Kinshasa) Gov ernment will hold 50 per cent of shares instead of the present 17 per cent. The company's headquarters will -move from Belgium to Kinshasa.. .Radio Kinshasa describes this as "the first victory in the battle for the economic independ ence of our country".
